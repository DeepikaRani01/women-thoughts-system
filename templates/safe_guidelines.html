Safe Space Guidelines 💜

This community is built on trust, empathy, and mutual respect. To keep it safe for all women, please follow these rules:

1. Be Kind and Supportive

We are all here for support. Avoid judgmental language, unsolicited advice unless asked, and any form of negativity.

2. Anonymity is a Right

Respect the privacy of those who choose to post anonymously. Never attempt to "dox" or identify someone who wishes to remain private.

3. No Harassment or Hate Speech

Any form of bullying, harassment, or hate speech based on identity will result in an immediate and permanent ban.

4. Verify for Credibility

While optional, gender verification helps build trust. We encourage all members to verify their gender via the camera tool during signup.

5. Reporting Violations

If you see a post that violates these rules, use the flag icon 🚩 to alert the admin team. We review every report.
